2	82	当电源告警（指示灯变为红色）时，请检查电源是否出现输出短路、输出过流、输出过压、温度过高等情况。	When the status LED is red, check whether the power module has encountered the following failures: output short circuit, output over-current, output over-voltage, or overheat.	
2	83	当输出短路、输出过流、温度过高等问题解决后，电源将自动恢复正常工作。	If you have detected and removed the output short circuit, output over-current, or overheat failures, the power module automatically restores.	
2	84	但当电源由于输出过压而告警时，会使电源进入锁死状态，此时电源不会自动恢复正常工作，请关闭电源开关并断开电源线，然后重新连接电源线打开电源开关。	However, if the power module encounters the output over-voltage failures, the power module will enter the deadlock sate, and cannot automatically restore after you remove the failures. To restore the power module in the deadlock state, turn off the power switch, unplug the power cord, plug the power cord, and then turn on the power switch.	
2	85	请不要频繁开关电源，每次开关的时间间隔应在30秒以上。	Do not frequently turn on and turn off the power module. The interval between each turning on and turn off operation should be longer than 30 seconds.	
2	86	断开电源输入端断路器后，电源指示灯会保持一段时间后才熄灭，此为正常现象。	After the circuit breaker that connects to a power module is off, it is normal that the LEDs on the power module last on for a while.	
2	87	电源模块选配原则	Power module configuration	
2	88	可以根据设备实际功耗来确定选配电源模块的数量N，根据供电情况灵活进行配电设计：	You can determine the number of power modules to be configured for the system according to the actual power consumption and power supply conditions.	
2	89	对于双电网输入配电的用户，选择N+N配置可以实现双输入冗余配电方式；	In an environment with two power sources, you can configure N+N redundant mode.	
2	90	对于单电网输入配电的用户，采取N+1或者N+N的配置，可以实现电源模块冗余配电；	In an environment with only one power source, you can configure N+1 or N+N redundant mode.	
2	91	无论采取何种供电方式，为了便于使用和维护，请为每个电源的输入都配置一个合适的断路器，和电源模块直接相连的每路断路器的额定通流能力要大于25A。	For easy usage and maintenance, be sure to configure a proper circuit breaker for each power input. The rated current of the circuit breaker must be more than 25 A.	
2	92	N的取值与设备电源槽位数有关，N+1或者N+N不大于电源槽位总数。	the value of N depends on the number of power module slots in the device. N+1 or N+N must not be more than the total number of power module slots.	
2	93	安装电源模块	Installing a power module	
2	94	PSR1200-A和PSR1200-D电源模块不能与其它不同型号的电源模块混插在同一台设备上，且PSR1200-A和PSR1200-D也不能在同一台设备上混插。	PSR1200-A and PSR1200-D cannot work with other models of power modules in the same device, and the PSR1200-A and PSR1200-D cannot work together in the same device.	
2	95	当电源模块温度超过正常工作温度时，电源将自动关闭，当温度恢复到正常范围后，电源将会自动开启。	When the temperature of the power module is higher than the upper limit of the operating temperature, the power module automatically shuts down. When the temperature falls into the normal range, the power module automatically starts.
